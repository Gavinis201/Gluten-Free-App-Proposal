Included Features

What is included

  • User authentication: account creation, secure sign-in, email verification, and forgotten password reset.
  • Role-based permissions: access levels for admins, approved contributors, paid users, and general users.
  • Contributor approval questions: onboarding questions provided by Anna, with admin review before users can review restaurants or upload images.
  • Payment access: subscription or one-time payment processing with paid-access logic through Stripe or PayPal.
  • Interactive park maps: custom park maps with editable restaurant pins and gluten-free dining locations.
  • Restaurant and menu pages: descriptions, hours, photos, prices, gluten-free status, tags, and gluten exposure notes.
  • Search and filters: search by park, restaurant, and menu item with filters for meal type, dedicated fryer, mobile ordering, and location.
  • Reviews and photo uploads: approved contributors can leave ratings, write reviews, upload images, and share gluten-free/celiac context.
  • Community updates: users can suggest changes or corrections that remain pending until admin approval.
  • Artwork and responsive design: custom artwork, icons, and layouts for phones, tablets, and desktop computers.

Private Owner Tools

Admin Content Management

  • Park Management: create, edit, delete, reorder, and upload park maps.
  • Map Management: place, move, edit, and remove restaurant pins visually on uploaded maps.
  • Restaurant Management: create, edit, archive, and manage restaurant information.
  • Menu Management: create, edit, archive, and organize menu items by restaurant and meal type.
  • Review Management: view, approve, remove, or moderate reviews and uploaded photos.
  • Contributor Approval Management: review answers to Anna's onboarding questions and approve users before they can post reviews or upload images.
  • Permissions Management: manage which users can access admin tools, paid content, review posting, image uploads, and contributor-only features.
  • Community Update Management: approve or reject suggested changes before they appear publicly.
  • Account Email Settings: configure the email address used for account verification and forgotten password messages.
